void DirektDSP::DirektFlexContainer::enableDragReorder ( bool  enable)

Enable or disable drag-to-reorder for DirektSection children.

When enabled, each DirektSection child receives a drag handle in its title bar and the container shows a drop-position indicator while the user drags. Only meaningful for Column-direction containers.
